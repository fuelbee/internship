INTERNSHIP

About Us
Fuelbee offers an internship program for developing young talents who are aspiring to become independent and contributing individuals in the society. We take up education as the centre focus to harness their talents and channelize their energy in a structured learning environment. They get hands-on experience in handling class, educating children and creating learning modules. Apart from gaining real world experience they also get to connect with like-minded youngsters who are passionate about teaching.

INTERNSHIP CATEGORIES

The Internship is offered in 3 formats

Paid Internship

Here the students are expected to enroll for the program by paying a nominal fee to gain the knowledge and experience FuelBee is offering.

Free Internship

Students can enroll in the program on merit basis to avail the free internship scheme. Their academic record, commitment towards social cause and passion for teaching will be tested as the benchmark.

Scholarship

For certain deserving candidates, the program is offered as a paid internship.
